
   To win a campaign, you need three M - Media, Money and Mojo. These are your ASSETS.
   Every turn, Media generates red News, Mojo generates blue Hype and Money generate green Cash.
   Spend News, Hype and Cash to get even more Media, Mojo and Money or to get more voters.
   Partisans can only be damaged if no Swing Voters are left or if the card strikes at Partisans.
   Otherwise, Swing Voters take damage first.
   The player who loses Partisans and Swing Voters first, loses the game.
   To play a card, slide it up.
   To discard a card, slide it down.
